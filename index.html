History

of Qeparo & Suga Region

Qeparo is a historic village in southern Albania, divided into Old Qeparo, nestled on a hillside, and New Qeparo, set along the Ionian Sea. With roots in ancient Illyrian civilizations, the village has been shaped by Roman, Byzantine, and Ottoman influences, making it a fascinating destination. Today, Qeparo is known for its olive groves, traditional stone architecture, and stunning seaside views.

Guest House Suga is located on a cliffside in the village and close to Porto Palermo, offering panoramic views of the Ionian Sea and convenient access to the area's historical landmarks and natural beauty.
